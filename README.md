# 🏦 FinBridge AI

### AI-Powered Alternative Credit Scoring Platform

> Bridging the gap between underserved borrowers and formal financial institutions using Explainable AI and alternative behavioral data.

---

# 📌 Overview

FinBridge AI is an AI-driven alternative credit scoring platform designed to help individuals and MSMEs who lack traditional credit history access formal loans digitally.

Traditional banking systems heavily depend on:

* CIBIL scores
* Previous loan history
* Formal income records

However, millions of people in India — including small business owners, gig workers, and rural borrowers — remain financially excluded despite being financially responsible.

FinBridge AI solves this problem by analyzing alternative behavioral and financial signals using Machine Learning and Explainable AI.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

Many individuals and MSMEs in India do not possess formal credit history, making it difficult for banks and financial institutions to assess their creditworthiness.

As a result:

* Genuine borrowers are rejected
* Financial inclusion decreases
* Banks face uncertainty and NPA risks

Our solution creates an alternative AI-powered credit assessment system that evaluates borrowers using consent-driven behavioral and financial data.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

FinBridge AI generates an alternative credit score using:

* 📱 Mobile bill payment consistency
* 🛒 E-commerce purchase behavior
* 📍 Geolocation stability
* 🧠 Psychometric risk assessment
* ⭐ Merchant ratings
* 💳 Bank cash flow patterns

The platform provides:

* Explainable AI decisions
* Consent-based data handling
* Fast digital loan assessment
* Transparent risk analysis
